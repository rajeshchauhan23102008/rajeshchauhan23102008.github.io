The Program is available only for new course purchase on GoGoodLearn Technology LLP. Any Registered user of GoGoodLearn Technology LLP, who initiate a referral, are defined as "Referrers." Those who purchase courses based upon a referral are defined as "Referees"

3. Eligibility

3.1 The Referrers is eligible for referral Amount only on the first transaction of the Referees.

3.2 The Referees must not Cancel or Refund the Course Order.

3.3 Only valid with full course fee payment in one go and not valid for Installment based payments.

4. Incentive Scheme

The mode of payment is subject to change, without notice.

4.1 For India:

Referrers will receive referral amount of Rs 1000 (One Thousand Indian Rupees INR).

The Referees will receive a discount of Rs 1000 (One Thousand Indian Rupees INR) from the total Course fee at the time of Payment.

4.2 For Rest of the world except India:

Referrers will receive referral amount of $10(Ten USD Dollar) via PayPal.

The Referees will receive a discount of $10(Ten USD Dollar) from the total Course fee at the time of Payment.

5. Process

5.1. Both Referrers and Referees need to First Register at our website https://www.gogoodlearn.com/

5.2. At the time of Course Fee Payment, Referees need to enter the Referrers Registered Email Id to avail "refer and earn" discount.

Note: Referrers will receive referral amount within 30 days after the Referees order has crossed the Refund period.
